Documentation, FAQs, and contact options

Frequently Asked Questions
How do I add a new order?
Click the "+ Add Order" button in the top right of the Dashboard or Orders page. Fill in customer name, product, amount, status and date, then click Save Order.
How do I export my data?
Click the "Export" button on the Dashboard or Orders page. A CSV file containing all your orders will be downloaded automatically.
How do I delete an order?
Go to the Orders page, find the order you want to remove, and click the red trash icon in the Actions column. You will be asked to confirm before deletion.
Is my data safe?
Yes. All data is stored in Firebase Firestore with authentication required. Only you can access your data — each account has its own isolated data collection.
How do I switch to light mode?
Click the moon/sun icon in the top navigation bar, or use the Dark Mode toggle in Settings → Preferences.
